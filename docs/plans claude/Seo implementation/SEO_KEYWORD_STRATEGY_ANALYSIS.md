## Executive Summary

The LabManager website demonstrates **strong foundational SEO with excellent metadata structure** but exhibits **significant under-optimization in primary and secondary keyword density throughout content**. The site ranks well for branded searches but shows **critical gaps in long-tail keyword coverage** essential for organic discovery in the Italian pastry software market.

**Overall SEO Score: 6.2/10** (Solid foundation requiring keyword distribution improvements)

**Key Findings:**
- Primary keyword density is 40% below best practice (0.15% vs. 0.5-1.5% target)
- Long-tail keywords almost completely absent from main page content
- Schema.org structured data is exceptional and well-implemented
- Metadata and open graph optimization exceeds industry standards
- FAQ content provides good long-tail coverage but remains siloed from homepage
- Critical opportunity to boost rankings through natural keyword redistribution
